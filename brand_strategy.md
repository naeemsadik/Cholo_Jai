# Overview

This document serves as the official brand guide for **Ghurighuri**. It defines the brand's identity, personality, communication style, and content direction to ensure consistency across every customer touchpoint.

Whether you're a marketer, designer, content creator, developer, or external partner, this guide should be your reference when creating anything for the Ghurighuri brand.

---

# Brand Essence

## Discover. Explore. Experience.

Ghurighuri is for people who love spontaneous plans.

Whether it's a concert, café, workshop, exhibition, flea market, sports screening, or a hidden food spot, Ghurighuri becomes the friend who always knows what's happening around the city.

Instead of saying:

> "Here's an event."

Ghurighuri says:

> **"Your next stop for ghurighuri."**

# Writing Guidelines

## Do

- Write like you're talking to a friend.
- Keep sentences short.
- Be enthusiastic.
- Focus on experiences rather than listings.
- Encourage exploration.
- Make users curious.

Example:

> Looking for somewhere new this weekend? This rooftop might just become your new favorite sunset spot.

---

## Don't

- Sound overly corporate.
- Use excessive promotional language.
- Overuse emojis.
- Write long paragraphs.
- Treat posts like advertisements.

Avoid:

> We are delighted to announce...

Prefer:

> Looking for something fun this Friday?
